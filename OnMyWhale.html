On My Whale

One of the primary reasons I love whales is their intelligence. Whale have specialized brain cells called spindle neurons that are associated with advanced abilities like recognizing, remembering and reasoning. Whales are mammals , like humans and are warm-blooded. They produce milk and nurse their young, similar to humans.

Some whales are very social, like killer whales, display personality traits asscociated with playfulness, cheerfulness and affection.My love for whales is in their connection to human history and culture.
